About Osborn Healthcare

Osborn Healthcare is built around the practice of Dr. Deepak Chandra Srivastava, MBBS, MD (Medicine), F.I.N. (Nephrology), offering patient-centered physician care and kidney-focused treatment in Gorakhpur.

According to Dr. Deepak’s official website, his background includes graduation and MD Medicine from JNMCH, Aligarh Muslim University, fellowship training in nephrology at Apollo Hospitals, New Delhi, and clinical experience at AIIMS Gorakhpur and Apollo Hospital, New Delhi.

His practice focuses on careful diagnosis, compassionate consultation, and procedures including dialysis catheter placement, central line insertion, renal biopsy, lumbar puncture, and bone marrow biopsy.

Our mission is to support better health through clinical excellence, trusted guidance, and accessible care for patients and families in Gorakhpur.
